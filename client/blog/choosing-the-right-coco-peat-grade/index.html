Choosing the right coco peat grade for your crop

EC, pH, buffering a quick guide for procurement teams sourcing coco peat for the first time.

When buying coco peat, three numbers matter most: EC (electrical conductivity), pH, and buffering. Washed coco peat with an EC below 0.5 mS/cm is suitable for nearly all crops. Buffered (calcium-treated) coco peat is recommended for sensitive species like tomatoes and gerbera. Always request a current QC report and a small sample before placing your first FCL order...
