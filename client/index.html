Why Coba Peat

Three decades of doing it right.

Every batch passes through our own facilities from harvest to QC to the loading bay. No outsourcing, no surprises.

  • Self-sufficient factory

    Own engineering department and machinery parts store keep production running without third-party delays.

  • Chartered chemists & QC lab

    In-house testing for EC, pH, moisture and physical properties before every shipment leaves the yard.

  • No-composting, ultra-lightweight

    Our patented drying process produces uniformly light blocks that expand cleanly with no off-odours.

  • Eco-friendly & biodegradable

    100% renewable coconut by-products, packaged with biodegradable materials wherever possible.
