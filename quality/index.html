Process

From husk to export six stages.

  1. 01

    Husk Selection

    Sourced from Sri Lanka's coconut triangle and graded by hand.

  2. 02

    Processing

    Mechanical de-fibring and sieving produces clean peat and chips.

  3. 03

    Drying

    No-composting, ultra-lightweight drying method uniform moisture, no odour.

  4. 04

    QC Testing

    In-house lab tests EC, pH, moisture and physical properties.

  5. 05

    Packing

    Compressed into blocks, briquettes or bags to client specification.

  6. 06

    Export

    Loaded at our own bay, trucked to Colombo Port, shipped FCL worldwide.
